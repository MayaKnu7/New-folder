Recipe: Dutch Stroopwafels

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 cup caramel syrup (for filling)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, yeast, and cinnamon.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter, brown sugar, and water together.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and knead the dough until smooth.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into circles. Bake for 10 minutes at 350°F (175°C).
  5. While the cookies are warm, cut them in half and fill with caramel syrup.
  6. Allow to cool before serving.
